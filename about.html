OUR STORY

Why we started Mala

As a team of designers and creatives, we started mala as we saw the need for functional replacement for bags and pockets. We want to create sustainable, locally manufactured products that are minimalist and timeless in design, while also creating a conscious community surrounding sustainable practices.

Our aims and goals

To educate about the fashion industry and the impact it has on the environment
To become no.1 chosen brand for sustainability
To achieve positive social impact within local communities
To design with function and simplicity in mind

Why you should shop Mala

You should shop at Mala because we design and make with function and practically in mind.

Our pocket bags have a variety of features:

  • Made with 0-waste pattern cutting
  • Pleated front panels for extra expansion
  • Loops for attachment
  • French seams for extra durability
  • Can be used on its own or with another belt
  • Minimalistic design that can be adaptable to suit different styles
  • Made from off-cut or locally sourced fabrics
